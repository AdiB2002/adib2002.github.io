About

Hi there, I'm Adi! I'm an undergrad studying Computer Science and Math at UT Austin. Currently, I'm part of the HCI Lab, where I'm fortunate to have my research advised by Amy Pavel. My recent work has focused on developing a video editing tool for short-form video creation. Previously, I spent a summer in the Meyers Lab, where I contributed to data collection, cleaning, and processing to support COVID-19 research.

I am largely interested in research involving Human-AI Interaction. This includes research that addresses problems humans face through effective system or model design. Similar to my prior work, I'm excited about exploring how AI can support creative domains such as writing, drawing, video creation, and music. This area requires a fine balance between AI support and user control. Additionally, I find research centered in our social systems to be interesting. It is cool seeing how we better understand human social behavior to improve our own interactions.

Some of my interests include rock climbing, Formula 1 (Mercedes fan), college football (big Texas fan), hanging out with friends, coffee shops, working on my balcony, and wearing my sandals (I like good weather).
